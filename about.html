About Us

The Journey of Nayss Sipss

Established in 2024, Nayss Sipss was founded following my return to Perlis after three enriching years of working in Putrajaya. We chose to set up our brand in Kampung Wai, recognizing its immense potential as a hidden gem and a unique destination for travelers seeking an authentic local experience. The name "Nayss" is a creative play on the word "Nice"—crafted to ensure that every sip you take feels exactly that: absolutely nice.

Operating Hours

Days: Thursday - Monday

Time: 5:00 PM - 7:30 PM

Closed on: Tuesday & Wednesday

What Makes Us Special

We specialize in the art of Vietnamese Coffee and premium Matcha. While many cafes in Perlis predominantly serve Arabica beans, we take pride in staying true to the bold and authentic heritage of Vietnamese coffee. Our commitment is to preserve the rich, traditional flavor profiles that offer our customers a truly distinctive caffeine experience.

Our Vision for the Future

Our ultimate goal is to transition into a cozy mini-cafe, creating a more comfortable and inviting atmosphere for our community to gather. This expansion will allow us to extend our operating hours, ensuring that the signature taste of Nayss Sipss is available for you to enjoy throughout the day.
