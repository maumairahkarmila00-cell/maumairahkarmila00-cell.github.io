Role & Contribution

  • Inventory & Logistics Management: Ensuring all ingredients and business logistics are fully stocked and ready for daily operations.

Contributions to Kampung Wai

Boosting Local Tourism

Nayss Sipss plays a pivotal role in promoting Kampung Wai, Kuala Perlis, as a must-visit destination for travelers. Beyond serving drinks, we provide a cozy and relaxing space for visitors to unwind while enjoying the scenic beauty and local charm of our village.

Challenges as an Entrepreneur

Overcoming Entrepreneurial Challenges

Our primary challenge lies in building a loyal customer base and maintaining consistent sales performance. We address this through proactive social media marketing and a steadfast commitment to beverage quality, ensuring that our customer growth remains steady and sustainable.
